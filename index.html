Common mental health disorders

Below is a list of the most common mental health disorders people suffer from.
Each card describes the various causes and symptoms of the listed disorder.

Anxiety

Common anxiety symptoms are:

1)Excessive worrying, panic
2)Restlessness, fatigue
3)Difficulty concentrating, irritability
3)Tense muscles
4)Trouble falling or staying asleep
5)Panic attacks
6)Avoiding social situations
7)Nausea, dizziness, shortness of breath

Depression

Common depression symptoms are:

1)Continuous sadness/hoplessness
2)Irritability and intolerance
3)No motivation, interest or energy
4)Not getting any enjoyment out of life
